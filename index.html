Learn

Short, visual explanations. No picks, certainty claims, or hype.

What is implied probability?

It translates American odds into the break-even win rate implied by the price.

Negative: p = |odds| / (|odds| + 100) Positive: p = 100 / (odds + 100)

What is vig / hold?

Books price both sides above 100% combined probability. The excess is the estimated hold.

hold = sum(implied probabilities) - 1

Why best line matters

Two books can imply different break-even rates for the same side. Better price lowers the hurdle.

edge estimate = fair no-vig probability - market implied probability
